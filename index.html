About

Hey! I'm Saayuj, a robotics engineer focused on the hardest part - getting robots to actually work when you deploy them in the real world.

I'm currently pursuing my MS in Robotics at Penn and did my Bachelor's (Honors) in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Bombay. Whether it's teaching robot arms to handle objects safely or building simulations that match reality, I focus on the messy problems between theory and deployment. That obsession led me to start Novara Robotics (more to come soon!).

Outside of my startup and coursework, I'm either at the gym or on the tennis court.
